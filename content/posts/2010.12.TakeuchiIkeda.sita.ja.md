### Abstract:

We argue the problem of determination of channel capacity from an information geometrical view point. Our main concern is analogue channels with peak power constraint. It is known that the channel capacity is typically achieved by a discrete input distribution in such setting. In this paper, we give an information geometrical view on such phenomena.
